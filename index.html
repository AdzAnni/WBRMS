Our Journey at MSU-Sulu

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Batch 2025 of Mindanao State University - Sulu represents a diverse group of talented individuals who have successfully completed their four-year academic journey.

Throughout our time at MSU-Sulu, we have developed strong technical skills in programming, software development, database management, and computer systems. Our curriculum has prepared us to tackle real-world challenges in the ever-evolving field of technology.

We are grateful to our dedicated faculty members, supportive families, and the MSU-Sulu administration for guiding us through this transformative educational experience.
